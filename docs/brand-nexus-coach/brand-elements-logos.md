# Nexus Coach Logos

> A clear, confident mark that communicates professional structure and human connection.

## Strategic Foundation

**Brand Alignment:** The Nexus Coach logo embodies The Guide — calm, authoritative, and approachable. The logomark represents the nexus (connection point) between coach and client, between structure and growth, between operational excellence and human transformation. The wordmark uses Plus Jakarta Sans Bold for geometric precision with a warm touch, reinforcing that coaching is both professional and personal.

## Logo Concept

### The Nexus Mark
The logomark is built on two overlapping elements that form a nexus point — representing the connection at the heart of every coaching relationship. The geometric, clean construction reflects the platform's operational precision (The Architect), while the overlapping forms suggest partnership and growth (The Guide). The mark uses Guide Teal as its primary color.

### Design Principles
- **Geometric clarity:** Clean lines, intentional curves, no decorative excess
- **Connection symbolism:** Intersecting forms that suggest partnership without being literal
- **Scalable simplicity:** Legible from favicon (16px) to billboard
- **Modern confidence:** Contemporary without following trends that will date quickly

## Logo Variations

### 1. Primary (Horizontal)
The full logo with the Nexus mark and wordmark side by side. Default for most contexts.

```
┌──────────────────────────────────┐
│                                  │
│   [Mark]  NEXUS COACH            │
│                                  │
└──────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Usage:** Marketing headers, website navigation, documents, presentations.

### 2. Stacked (Vertical)
The mark above the wordmark. For square or constrained spaces.

```
┌──────────────────┐
│                  │
│     [Mark]       │
│   NEXUS COACH    │
│                  │
└──────────────────┘
```

**Usage:** App icons, social media profiles, compact layouts.

### 3. Logomark Only
The Nexus mark alone. For contexts where the brand is already established.

```
┌──────────┐
│          │
│  [Mark]  │
│          │
└──────────┘
```

**Usage:** Favicons, app icons, watermarks, loading states, small-format applications.

### 4. Wordmark Only
The brand name in styled text. For minimal contexts or when paired with other brand elements.

```
┌──────────────────────────┐
│                          │
│   NEXUS COACH            │
│                          │
└──────────────────────────┘
```

**Usage:** Email signatures, footer treatments, co-branding situations.

## Incorrect Usage

The following treatments compromise the logo's integrity and must be avoided.

1. **Don't stretch or distort** — Maintain original proportions at all times
2. **Don't rotate** — The logo should always appear level
3. **Don't change the colors** — Use only approved color variations
4. **Don't add effects** — No drop shadows, gradients, bevels, or glows
5. **Don't outline** — The logo is not a stroke treatment
6. **Don't place on busy backgrounds** — Ensure sufficient contrast and legibility
7. **Don't rearrange elements** — The mark and wordmark have fixed spatial relationships
8. **Don't crop the mark** — The logomark must always appear complete
9. **Don't change the typeface** — The wordmark uses Plus Jakarta Sans Bold exclusively
10. **Don't add taglines to the logo** — Taglines are separate elements with their own spacing rules
11. **Don't animate without approval** — Motion treatments require brand team review
12. **Don't place below minimum size** — Small logos become illegible and lose their impact

## Co-Branding

When the Nexus Coach logo appears alongside partner or client logos:
- Maintain equal visual weight between logos
- Separate logos with a vertical divider (1px, Slate 200) or generous spacing (minimum 2x clear space)
- Never modify the Nexus Coach logo to match partner styling
- The Nexus Coach logo should appear first (left or top) when we are the primary brand

## Usage Guidelines

### Do
- Use the SVG version whenever possible for crisp rendering
- Maintain minimum clear space in all contexts
- Choose the color variation that provides highest contrast against the background
- Use the Stacked variation for square or compact contexts
- Use the Logomark Only for established-brand contexts (app icons, favicons, watermarks)

### Don't
- Recreate the logo from scratch — always use approved files
- Use low-resolution raster files when vector is available
- Place the logo in a position that could be confused with navigation
- Use the full Primary logo in spaces smaller than 120px wide
- Combine the logo with other graphic elements or icons
