# iFoundry Media Colors

> Colors forged from the intersection of engineering precision and creative ambition.

## Strategic Foundation

**Brand Alignment:** iFoundry's color palette reflects The Builder's bold confidence and The Sage's depth of expertise. The dual-primary system — Forge Blue and Ignite Orange — mirrors the logo's blue nodes and red-orange energy lines: structured intelligence meeting transformative action. Deep Iron Slate anchors the palette with the weight and seriousness of a technology foundry, while clean whites and cool grays provide the breathing room that lets engineering content stay readable and focused.

## Color Ratios & Hierarchy

### 60-30-10 Rule
- **60% Neutrals:** White, slates — clean engineering canvas, readability, breathing room
- **30% Primary:** Forge Blue, Iron Slate — brand authority and structural depth
- **10% Accent:** Ignite Orange, secondary colors — energy, action, and transformation

### Application Priority
1. **Forge Blue** for primary actions, interactive elements, and brand identity
2. **Ignite Orange** for the most important CTAs, energy moments, and conversion points
3. **Iron Slate** for dark backgrounds, headings, navigation, and structural weight
4. **Neutrals** for backgrounds, body content, and structural elements
5. **Semantic colors** for status and feedback only — never decorative

### Button Hierarchy

| Button Type | Background | Text | Border |
|-------------|------------|------|--------|
| Primary CTA | Ignite Orange | White | None |
| Primary Action | Forge Blue | White | None |
| Secondary | Transparent | Forge Blue | Forge Blue |
| Ghost | Transparent | Slate 600 | None |
| Destructive | Alert Red | White | None |

## Accessibility

### Contrast Ratios (WCAG 2.1)

| Combination | Ratio | Level |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| Iron Slate on White | 16.7:1 | AAA ✓ |
| Forge Blue on White | 5.1:1 | AA ✓ |
| Slate 600 on White | 5.7:1 | AA ✓ |
| White on Forge Blue | 5.1:1 | AA ✓ |
| White on Iron Slate | 16.7:1 | AAA ✓ |
| White on Ignite Orange | 3.3:1 | AA Large ✓ |
| Iron Slate on Ignite Orange | 4.2:1 | AA Large ✓ |
| Ignite Orange on White | 3.3:1 | AA Large ✓ |

### Guidelines
- **Body text:** Use Slate 600+ on light backgrounds for AA compliance
- **Large text (18pt+):** Can use 3:1 minimum
- **Interactive elements:** Minimum AA compliance (4.5:1)
- **CTA buttons:** Use White text on Ignite Orange for large text; use White on Forge Blue for all text sizes
- **Don't rely on color alone:** Always pair with icons, text labels, or patterns
