Common questions

Quick answers to things we're often asked before a first conversation.

What types of companies do you work with?
We work with both startups building their first product and established companies modernizing existing systems. Our sweet spot is teams who need a technology partner — not just a vendor.
How does the partnership model work?
Depending on the project, we offer co-ownership, equity participation, and service-based engagements. We build like we own it — and in many cases, we literally do. We'll explore the right model during our discovery call.
What's a typical project timeline?
Most projects run 6 to 14 months from kickoff to launch. We scope deliberately during discovery and architect for iteration, so you see meaningful progress early — not a big reveal at the end.
Do you work with existing codebases?
Absolutely. Our modernization practice is built around incrementally improving legacy systems — preserving what works, replacing what doesn't, and avoiding the risky "big bang" rewrite.
